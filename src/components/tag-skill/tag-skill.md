`TagSkill` is the variant of `Tag` for Mavenlink skills.
Refer to the [Tag documentation](#/Components/Tag) for more information.

```js
<TagSkill id="egg" name="Egg" />
```

<TagSkill id="bacon" name="Bacon" level={1} />
